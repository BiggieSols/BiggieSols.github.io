Minzio


Personality Profiles for the Workplace

Work Better with your Colleagues

Minzio is a LinkedIn-integrated personality assessment with workplace-focused results. The service gives specific recommendations on how to work with each of your colleagues based on an evaluation of their personality type.

I personally handle all business operations for Minzio. Web development, product, UI/UX, marketing, customer development, etc.

Currently in early testing with several Bay Area companies. Continuing to build out the web product based on early user research. Full launch targeted for August 2014.

Single-page Backbone app consuming RESTful Rails API. LinkedIn API Integration to gather user information and support in-network messaging.
